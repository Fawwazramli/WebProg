ABOUT US

MUSE Group Asia (M) Sdn. Bhd

Over the past three years, MUSE Group Asia (Malaysia) has organised a number of international class mass participation events such as the PROTON HSN21KM 2018, ATM Half Marathon 2017 and JKR Roadrunners in 2017. Meanwhile in Thailand, we are the people behind the inaugural Bangkok Midnight Marathon, Phukethon & Spinman in 2017. As we shifted gear in early 2018, with the mission of bringing quality running events to local runners, MUSE Group Asia will now consolidate all our mass participation events under a new regional brand called MOVE. *MOVE* is entrusted with the vision of bringing more innovative and quality to our mass participation events across the region, and runners can look forward to more interesting and positive running experience at all move events. Make way for MOVE. A new Mass Participation brand under MUSE GROUP ASIA New Year. New Look. New Spirit. Quality Assured.
